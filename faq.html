FAQ — Online Bingo Caller 1–90

Here are the most common questions about this free 90-ball bingo caller. If you still need help, you can contact me using the Contact page.

Is this online bingo caller free to use?

Yes. The online bingo caller 1–90 is free to use. You can open the website and start calling numbers instantly.

Can numbers repeat during a game?

No. This tool draws each number from 1 to 90 only once per game (no repetition). Once all numbers are drawn, the game naturally ends.

Does it work on mobile phones and tablets?

Yes. It runs in modern browsers on desktop, laptop, tablet, and mobile. For larger groups, it’s best to mirror the screen to a TV.

How do I use it for a pub quiz or a bingo night?

Open the Home page, connect your device to a TV/projector, and use speakers. Decide the rules (one line / two lines / full house), then press Draw Number to call numbers one-by-one.

How do I mute sound and voice?

On the Home page, use the Sound on/off button. It mutes both the chime and the voice callouts.

Is the voice always British English?

The tool uses your device/browser speech settings. By default it aims for British English, but the exact voice can vary between browsers.

Can I undo the last number?

Yes. Press Undo to revert the last called number. This is useful if you clicked by mistake.

Can I reset the game?

Yes. Press Reset to start a fresh 1–90 bingo game.

Tip: If you’re hosting a group event, keep a steady pace and don’t call too fast — it makes the game more enjoyable.
