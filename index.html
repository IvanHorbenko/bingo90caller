What is a 90-ball bingo caller?

A 90-ball bingo caller is a tool that draws random numbers from 1 to 90 without repetition and announces them for players. It replaces the traditional physical bingo machine and makes it easy to host bingo nights at home, in pubs, offices or community events.

This online caller works in your browser, so you don't need to install anything. Just connect your screen and speakers and you are ready to run a full 90-ball bingo game.
